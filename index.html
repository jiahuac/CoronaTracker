CoronaTracker

Tired of trying to find the latest, up-to-date information? CoronaTracker offers a simple, graphical view of the growth and spread of the virus.

Get beta access

Track the Coronavirus in real time.

Track the 2019 novel-Coronavirus (Covid-19) in real time, with information pulled from governmental sources and outlets. A list view shows the number of confirmed cases by country, province, and city.

Track the live spread of the virus.

Track the spread of the virus on a map, showing a graphical representation of the spread and severity of the disease. The map includes city-level data reporting for cities in China.

Many new features are coming to CoronaTracker, including Dark Mode support, Notification support, and many more.
